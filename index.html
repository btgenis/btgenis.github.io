Most of what we see are neither important nor interesting: but the way we see them — that’s a different story. When we come across a self-publication, what excites us are not the images themselves, but how that person sees the world and chooses to frame it. The way even the most ordinary person experiences the world—and, more importantly, the way they choose to share it—holds something fascinating. And as the allure of the way we see it exceeds that of the picture itself, for some of us, the way we present it is far more interesting than the content itself: This website is, essentially, a zine.
